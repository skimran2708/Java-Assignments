StringBuilder class :

    - The classname is in upper camel case/Pascal case.
    - The method names are in lower camel case except the constructors. Ex. append(), capacity().
    - methods are public.
    - methods name are verbs.
    - variables are also in lower camel case, ex. offset, endIndex, startIndex.
    - variables does not start with special characters.
    - Doesn't contain/avoids one-character variables.



LocalDateTime class :

    - The classname is in upper camel case/Pascal case.
    - The method names are in lower camel case except the constructors. Ex. get(), format().
    - methods are public.
    - methods name are verbs.
    - Constants are in capital letters and follows snake case.
    - variables are also in lower camel case, ex. days, time, formatter.
    - variables does not start with special characters.
    - Doesn't contain/avoids one-character variables.
